Profile template

For all people that share their profile on this site, we'd like for them to have the following:
  • Picture of yourself (if you're comfortable adding one)
  • Years of experience in technology
  • Provide perspective on your background – what got you to here?
  • What's your reason–impact, money, flexibility?
  • What are some of your strategies for success–what do you use to succeed?
  • What are some resources for people who want to learn from your experience–websites you use, communities you're a part of?

Guidelines

* 1-2 paragraphs is great here. We're looking to concise, but we're open to longer stories if your experience warrants one. Consider linking to bigger stories on Medium or Dev.to * The personal narrative is more important than external links. Help people see themselves in your story.
